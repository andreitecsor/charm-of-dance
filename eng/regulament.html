The International Dance Festival «The Charm of Dance»

8th edition – May 21st 2016
2016

FIRST PART – THE DANCE COMPETITION

 

Art. 1.

The competition “The Charm of Dance” is open to all high school students aged between 15 and 19 years old who are not enrolled in vocational art schools from Romania or other countries.


Art. 2. 

a) Registration will be made by e-mail to the following address: chiarchim@yahoo.com Registration deadline: 15th APRIL 2016
b) Entries must include all of the following information:


> Name of the school
> Name of the team
> Section where participation is intended – according to Art. 3
> Duration of the performance
> Name of the team coaching teacher/choreographer
> Number of members in the team
> Signature of the high school’s principal, signature of the team coach.


c) The registration form must be accompanied by a portfolio presenting > the team’s activity and achievements for the past 3 years ( on CD) > two photographs of the team > a table listing the team members - their names, personal identification code number and signature - also signed by the school principal and by the team coach.
d) The organizers reserve the right to select the participants by assessing the portfolios forwarded by the coordinators. The selected teams will be announced by April 15th 2016.
e) A high school may enter only one team per competition section.


Art. 3.

The competition will include the following sections :


> Mixt folk dances– from district of origin - maximum performance time - 6 min. minimum number of pairs per team – 6
> Girls/Boys folk dance – from district of origin - maximum performance time 5 min. ; minimum number of dancers per team– 12
> Traditional national folk dances – maximum performance time 5 min.; minimum number of dancers per team - 12
> Modern dance – maximum performance time - 5 min.; minimum number of dancers per team - 10
> Cheerleaders – maximum performance time – 5 minutes; minimum number of members per team – 12


Art. 4.

The musical accompaniment for the folk dances may be live or recorded, must use traditional instruments and must be presented/sent to the organizers in MP3 form.

 

Art. 5.

The team members must be students of the educational institution registered for the competition. Teachers, coaching teachers, choreographers or any other persons who are not students of the registered school will not be accepted.

 

Art. 6.

The jury:

a) will be composed of specialists in the domain, representatives of the Mayoralty, of the Teleorman County Centre for the Preservation and Promotion of Folk Culture, of the Teleorman School Inspectorate, of the project partners and sponsors and two representatives of the Teleorman County Students’ Council
b) will be composed of an odd number of judges

 

Art. 7.

The following criteria will be considered when judging a team’s performance:
a) choreographic quality ( composition)
b) performance quality ( technical mastery)
c) artistic expression
d) timing; concordance between movement and music
e) authenticity
f) costumes
g) quality of the musical accompaniment (live music will be a tie-breaking criterion)

 

Art. 8.

Scoring
a) The judges will score the teams according to the adjudication criteria mentioned above.
b) Points will be awarded from 1 to 10 for each criterion
c) A team’s final score is the sum of the points received for each criterion
d) If at the end of the competition two or more teams have the same score, the chairperson of the
jury will make the tie-breaking decision.

 

Art. 9.

a) The teams will perform in the order established by the organizers, which will be announced in advance.
b) The competition starts at 11:00 on May 21st 2016.
c) The musical accompaniment must be submitted/sent as a digital MP3 format recording by 21st. 05. 2016, no later than 45 minutes prior to the start of the competition.

 

Art. 10.

Awards:
a) The jury will award one 1st prize, one 2nd prize, one 3rd prize and two mentions for each section of the competition, excepting the situations when two or more participants obtain the same score without any possibility of tie-breaking, in which case more prizes/mentions can be awarded for equal scores, without exceeding the total number of prizes and mentions. The decision regarding the awarding of several 1st, 2nd 3rd prizes or mentions belongs to the competition jury.
b) The total number of awards ( 1st, 2nd, 3rd prizes and mentions) cannot exceed 25% of the number of participating teams/ensembles for each section.
c) The special prizes will be those awarded by the sponsors and project partners.
d) All participants will receive participation diplomas attested by the Teleorman School Inspectorate, Alexandria Mayoralty, the A. I. Cuza National College .

 

Art. 11.

The organizing institution issues documents certifying the teachers' participation in the organization of the festival and acknowledging the partners' and sponsors' contributions

 

Art. 12.

Teams can be expelled from the competition in the following situations:

a) Failure to appear for the Opening Ceremony and Parade, 1 hour before the competition starts.
b) Attempted fraud;
c) Serious violation of the present rules by the team members
d) Serious misconduct of the team supporters.

SECOND PART - THE PRESENTATION OF TRADITIONAL COSTUMES CONTEST

Art.1.

a) The participating teams/ensembles will enter the contest with two traditional costumes (boy and girl), specific to the ethnographic area they represent.
b) Registration will be made by e-mail to the following address: chiarchim@yahoo.com Registration deadline: 15 APRIL 2016
c) Entries must include all of the following information:

> Name of the school
> the folkloric region they represent
> the competitors' full names
> Name of the team coaching teacher/choreographer
> Signature of the school principal, signature of the coaching teacher

 

Art.2.

The costumes will be worn and presented by a couple of dancers from a participating team/ensemble.

 

Art. 3.

The couple of dancers will perform as a demonstration a traditional folk dance specific to the region they represent. Maximum performance time – 2 min.

 

Art. 4.

The jury
a) will be composed of specialists in the domain, representatives the Teleorman County Centre for the Preservation and Promotion of Folk Culture, of the Teleorman County Museum, Alexandria Children's Palace
b) will be composed of an odd number of judges

 

Art. 5.

Adjudication criteria:

- age of the costume
- costume authenticity
- completeness of the costume
- costume presentation
- quality of the dance performance

 

Art.6.

Scoring:
a) The judges will score the teams according to the adjudication criteria mentioned above.
b) Points will be awarded from 1 to 10 for each criterion
c) The final score for each costume is the sum of the points received
d) If at the end of the competition two or more contestants have the same score, the chairperson of the jury will make the tie-breaking decision

 

Art.7.

Awards:
a) The jury will award one 1st prize, one 2nd prize, one 3rd prize and two mentions, excepting the situations when two or more participants obtain the same score, without any possibility of tie-breaking, in which case more prizes/mentions can be awarded for equal scores.The final decision regarding the number of awards belongs to the chairperson of the jury.
b) All participants will receive participation diplomas attested by the Teleorman School Inspectorate , Alexandria Mayoralty, the A. I. Cuza National College

THIRD PART – FINAL DISPOSITIONS

 

Art.1

a) The number of participants for each educational institution will be set by the organizers proportionate with the number of section they have registered to participate in and depending on the availability of accommodation and meals.
b) there will be 2-day accommodation for the participants coming form a distance that exceeds 250 km, while those coming from other counties will receive accommodation for one day, depending on availability.
c) registration is free.
d) organizers cannot provide transportation for participants
